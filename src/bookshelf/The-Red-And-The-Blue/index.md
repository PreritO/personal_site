I would highly recommend this book to anyone who has ever wondered how American politics has gotten so divided. Believe it or not, it wasn't always this way and even as recently as the late 80's, it wasn't uncommon for Democrat senators and house members to publically support a Republican president. The 90's were a transformative decade and Kornacki does an absolutely stellar job in recounting the incidents and people involved in deciding how American politics would be shaped for decades to come.


Rating: 4/5
