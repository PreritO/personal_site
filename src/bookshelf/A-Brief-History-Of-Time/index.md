I can't say enough good things about this book. If you've ever had even a slight interest in physics or engineering, this is a must read book. Hawking (thankfully) distills the information into analogies and concepts that make even the most advanced material a pleasure to read. I now know how little I know about physics and can bet that I cant't even begin to be able to decipher the math behind all the theories he discusses but his narrative regarding the evolution of physics is tremendous. It was hard to put this book down for me. I know that I'll go back to this book a few times in my life because of how magnificently it was written.


Rating: 5/5
